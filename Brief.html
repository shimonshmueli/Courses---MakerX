// The Mission

You don't just build a robot — you conceive the idea, design the personality and the body, build the electronics and firmware, and then pitch and sell it. Four weeks. One team. One product you own end-to-end.

Design Your Robot's Soul

Your robot isn't just a device — it has a personality. Before you solder a wire, ask: who is this robot? What does it feel like to talk to it? Shape, sound, language, and expression should all answer that question.
