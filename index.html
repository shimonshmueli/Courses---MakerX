// The Mission  ·  课程使命

Conceive it. Design it.
Build it. Pitch it.

构思 · 设计 · 制造 · 展示

Maker-X is an intensive 4-week project course where cross-disciplinary teams build AI-powered robotic companions from scratch — hardware, firmware, personality, and pitch. Instruction in Chinese & English.

01 💡 Conceive Identify a real user and a genuine need. Define the personality and the purpose of your robot before touching any hardware.
02 ✏️ Design Shape, sound, language, expression — every detail of the robot's soul. Prototype rapidly and iterate without fear.
03 🔧 Build Solder, code, 3D-print, and integrate. Electronics, firmware, enclosure — one complete, working product.
04 🎤 Pitch It Present your product to real university guests. A working demo, a poster, a story — this is your public debut.
// Course Objectives  ·  课程目标

What you'll walk away with

你将收获什么

Maker-X is about more than building a robot. It's about becoming someone who can think, collaborate, and create at the intersection of technology and design.

🤝
Cross-discipline Collaboration
Work in teams that span engineering, design, and beyond. Learn to communicate across disciplines, divide work strategically, and ship something greater than the sum of its parts.
🧠
Confidence with AI & LLM Tools
Go from curious to capable. You'll integrate large language models into real hardware — learning how to prompt, tune, and deploy AI as a core part of a product.
🎨
Design Thinking in Practice
Empathy, creativity, rapid prototyping, iteration, and communication — not as theory, but as daily practice. You'll learn by doing, and the product is the proof.

// Core Technology  ·  核心技术

What you'll build with

你将使用的技术与工具

Every team gets the same foundation. What you build on top of it is entirely yours.

You will use a variety of AI coding tools: Claude Code, Codex, and others.

🔩
Hardware
ESP32-based boards as the brain — Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, and enough compute for real-time AI interaction.

Paired with displays, sensors, cameras, and motors to give your robot awareness and expression.

The body? 3D-modeled and printed — designed by you, from scratch.
⚙️
Frameworks
ESP-Claw — a purpose-built firmware framework for ESP32 AI robots, handling voice, display, and LLM integration so you can focus on what matters: your idea.

OpenRouter — a unified API gateway that lets you switch between any LLM with a single interface, no vendor lock-in.
🤖
Large Language Models
Your robot's intelligence comes from state-of-the-art LLMs. We'll work with leading models including:

Qwen  ·  DeepSeek  ·  Claude

…and others. You'll learn how to prompt, tune, and integrate them into real hardware products.
