// The Mission

Conceive it. Design it.
Build it. Sell it.

Maker-X is an intensive 4-week project course where cross-disciplinary teams build AI-powered robotic companions from scratch — hardware, firmware, personality, and pitch. Instruction in Chinese & English.

01 💡 Conceive Identify a real user and a genuine need. Define the personality and the purpose of your robot before touching any hardware.
02 ✏️ Design Shape, sound, language, expression — every detail of the robot's soul. Prototype rapidly and iterate without fear.
03 🔧 Build Solder, code, 3D-print, and integrate. Electronics, firmware, enclosure — one complete, working product.
04 🚀 Sell Demo and pitch to real university guests. A poster, a product, a story — this is your public debut.
// Core Technology

What you'll build with

Every team gets the same foundation. What you build on top of it is entirely yours.

🧠
Brain — ESP32-S3
Microcontroller with integrated LCD display, Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, and enough compute to run the whole show.
☁️
Cloud LLM API
Connect your robot to a large language model over Wi-Fi. Your robot isn't scripted — it thinks.
👁️
Sensing
Camera, heart rate, GSR — or whatever makes your concept real. Pick the sensors that fit your user story.
🔊
Voice I/O
Speech synthesis and natural voice input. Your robot speaks, listens, and responds in real time.
🖨️
3D-Printed Body
Design and print a custom enclosure — up to 180 × 180 × 180 mm. Shape is part of the personality.
✦
Your X Factor
Maker-X leaves room for the unexpected. If it makes your robot more yours, it's fair game.
